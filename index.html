Investment Tips & Education

Start Early

The power of compounding works best when you start early. ‚Ç¶10,000 invested at 25 is worth more than ‚Ç¶50,000 invested at 40.

Diversify

Don't put all your eggs in one basket. Spread investments across stocks, bonds, real estate, and cash equivalents.

Consistency Beats Timing

Regular monthly investments (naira-cost averaging) often outperform trying to time the market perfectly.

Emergency Fund First

Always maintain 3-6 months of expenses in a liquid savings account before investing heavily.
